## Fair Use Notice

MedKitt is an educational tool for healthcare professionals. Our use of medical literature, guidelines, and educational images constitutes fair use under 17 U.S.C. § 107:

1. **Purpose:** Educational, non-commercial clinical decision support
2. **Nature:** Factual, published scientific/medical information
3. **Amount:** Summarized portions, not complete reproductions
4. **Market Effect:** Does not substitute for original publications
